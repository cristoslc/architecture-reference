                                                          A Brief Overview
                                            Farmacy Food is a tech-enabled food startup that takes
                                            the “Let food be thy medicine” quote literally and
                                            creates tasty meals around peoples’ dietary needs.
                                            Our mission is to make health and wellness radically
                                           affordable and accessible. Towards that end, we have
                                          recently expanded to offer Smartfridges, a glass front
                                        refrigerated vending machine, where your residents can
                                      conveniently access healthy food 24/7. It's almost like having
                                    a mini (2ft X 2ft) healthy restaurant in your apartment building!



Building a Healthy & Vibrant Community
More and more people want to eat healthier. However, the time it takes to do the proper research
to understand what is truly nutritious, gather materials to prepare meals, and ﬁnally craft those
meals in a satisfactory way is time prohibitive. With our team of chefs and nutritionists we create
healthy, locally sourced meals that are delicious and affordably priced to make it easy for people to
eat healthy consistently.
After we install a Smartfridge, we take care of every detail from re-stocking, maintenance, etc. and
leverage technology to ensure quality control and inventory levels are properly maintained.



Convenient & Contactless
Our Smartfridges provide a “Grab & Go” option within a
strategic location within your establishment. Tenants will be able
to access with their debit/credit card or they can order online
and effortlessly walk down to pick up their meal. Tenants have
quick and easy access to healthy food, while being socially
distant.



Our Offer to You
Moving and unpacking can be as exciting as it is stressful. We
want to assist in making that special move-in day more pleasant
by providing a free meal coupon for new tenants to be used on
move-in day or their ﬁrst week in their new home!
We hope that you see tremendous value in offering this unique
amenity to your tenants and that you’ll join us in helping to make
health and wellness more affordable and accessible!
