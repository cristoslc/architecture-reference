Bullet-riddled Corpse
Outlines should never have a single point at
an indent level; don’t create bullet points that
     have only one sub-bullet. Bullets in
 presentations mimic outlines and it “looks”
   grammatically incorrect to have orphan
                   bullets.
              Why So Many Bullets?
•   Both presenters and audience expect it.

•   Title + Bullets is often the default template.

•   Inexperienced speaker’s rely on bullets as speaker notes

•   Easy to bang together in a conference room while > 4 people are
    talking
